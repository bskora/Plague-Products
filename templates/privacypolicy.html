Privacy Policy

Last updated: 03/02/2026

This page explains how we collect and use data. For website usage rules, permissions, and legal terms, please review our Terms of Service.

1. Overview

Plague Products ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our website, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We are committed to keeping data collection limited, transparent, and secure.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require account registration and we do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data through this website.

We may collect limited technical and usage information through Google Analytics, which can include:

  • IP address (which may be shortened or anonymized where available),
  • browser and device type,
  • pages viewed and time spent on pages,
  • referring websites, and
  • general interaction and performance data.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • understand how visitors use our website,
  • improve content, usability, and site performance, and
  • monitor and protect the reliability and security of our services.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our site may use cookies or similar technologies provided by Google Analytics. These technologies help us understand traffic and usage patterns. You can control cookies through your browser settings and/or opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share limited data with service providers that help us operate and improve our website (for example, analytics providers). Those providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies, including Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain analytics information only for as long as reasonably necessary for reporting, security, and service improvement purposes, or as required by applicable law.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no online system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data (such as access, deletion, objection, or restriction). You may also disable cookies through your browser settings and use analytics opt-out tools.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

This policy is provided for general informational purposes and does not constitute legal advice.
