Code.Net

Enter a world in 2090 where all systems have been digitalized. The government is training their workforce to combat hackers through a social deduction game. Connect together with friends and family and score as many points as possible using actions and deception.

Features

Competitive

Play multiple matches using the point system in order to earn enough points for victory.

Teamwork

Players that play their roles incorrectly will jeopardize their own individual score.

Replayability

Every match shuffles roles, actions, objectives, and logs to keep the game fresh.

Unique

Without physical cards, complex, creative, and role specific actions are now possible.

Longevity

A digitalized board game allows for new features, content, and improvements overtime.

Multiplayer

Preform actions from the comfort of your own phone with no intrusion or sharing of your device.

Automation

Have faster and more fun games and less human error, forgetting, and arguing.

Visual

Always have the board and a guide available and visible to you at all times.
