Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11/4/2024

Introduction

Welcome to Plague Products. Your privacy matters to us, and we are dedicated to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to keep it secure.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information via Google Analytics to help us understand and improve your experience on our website. This may include non-identifiable details such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and the duration of your visit.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected through Google Analytics is used to enhance our services by analyzing usage trends and gaining insights into how our users interact with our content. This data is aggregated and anonymized, ensuring your personal identity is never compromised.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. However, Google Analytics may utilize cookies or similar tracking technologies to collect and process certain information as described in Google's Privacy Policy. Please refer to their policy for more details.

Security

We take your security seriously and employ industry-standard measures to protect any information that is transmitted. However, please keep in mind that no internet transmission is entirely risk-free, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have control over your data and can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking at any time. You can do this by using tools such as the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.
