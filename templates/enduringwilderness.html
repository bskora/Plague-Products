Features

Gathering

Transform your steps into valuable resources. Equip a tool to discover nodes and unlock a mini-game that determines the size and rarity of your rewards.

Dynamic Weather

Immerse yourself in a weather system that mimics real-world patterns, with seasons, time, temperature, humidity, and moon phases shaping events and activities.

Skills

Earn experience with every action and level up to boost your skills, becoming more effective across all activities.

Crafting

Craft items using gathered materials, with their tier shaped by what you collect. Each craft involves multiple stages where a shifting bar determines your progress, and the final stage decides the outcome. In-game skills improve your odds, but failure comes at a cost.

Item Degradation

Items wear down with use and break permanently when fully depleted. Better items and higher skills reduce the chance of breakage.
