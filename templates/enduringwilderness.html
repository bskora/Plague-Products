Implemented Features

Resource Gathering

Take steps and earn rewards! Cover some distance to collect resources, and with the right tools, swipe away at bigger nodes for greater rewards.

Dynamic Weather

Feel the world shift around you! Seasons change, temperatures rise and fall, and moon phases shape events—every moment brings new challenges and discoveries.

Character Progression

Grow stronger with every action! Earn experience, level up, and master your skills to become more efficient and effective in everything you do.

Crafting

Transform materials into useful creations! What you gather shapes what you can craft, with progress unfolding step-by-step. Sharpen your skills to boost success—but beware, every failure has its price.

Item Degradation

Watch your items wear down with use and break for good if fully depleted. Invest in better gear and sharpen your skills to slow wear and avoid costly breakage.

Salvaging

Recover materials from damaged items! Use your skills to get the most out of every worn-down piece, ensuring nothing goes to waste.
